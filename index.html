What is Recycle it all about?

Recycle It connects recyclable waste generators to recycling companies, and reward our users that drop waste with incentives.

How do I get rewarded for disposing my waste

For every time you drop recyclable waste, you earn points which can be redeemed for household items

How often should I gather up my waste?
