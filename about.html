About us
A few words about us
Who we are & What we do

We are a startup seeking to make sure that waste is being disposed properly. We believe in SDG number 13 which seeks to make sure that the environment is a habitable place for us all. Climate change is real and at Recycle It, we are driving this undersatnding to make sure that both the old and young are involved in managaing waste. We are pioneering social change using the environment, by allowing people capture value from their waste. Watse is gold, why waste it!

Recycle It is a service company that connects people/households with recyclable wastes to companies who need these wastes. We are the bridge and the middleman between the waste generators and the companies who need them. We hope to enhance a sustainable recycling service while sensitizing the public on the importance of recycling for environmental sustainability gained from reduction in pollution and diseases.

We can make sourcing for recyclable waste easy for your company.
Are you a company into recycling and you need a better network of getting raw materials. We are here to help.
